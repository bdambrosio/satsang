About This Site

Spirit

This is a contemplative tool inspired by the teachings of Bhagavan Sri Ramana Maharshi. It is not a simulation of Bhagavan, not a substitute for teachers or direct inquiry, and makes no claim to authority. It exists only to make these teachings more accessible.

AI Transparency

Responses are generated by a language model drawing on Bhagavan's source texts. They may sometimes miss the mark. The teachings themselves are what matter; this is just a pointer.

Sources

All texts are drawn from public domain sources, including Project Gutenberg. Specific works include Nan Yar? (Who Am I?), Ulladu Narpadu (Forty Verses on Reality), and Upadesa Undiyar (Thirty Verses on Instruction), among others.

Feedback

This project welcomes feedback, especially from scholars and practitioners of Bhagavan's teachings. If something here misrepresents the teachings or could be improved, we would like to hear from you at bruce@tuuyi.com.
